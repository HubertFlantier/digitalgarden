# Jocko Style Warmup
1.  Round 0
	- Hang bar (10-15 seconds)
	- Plank (10-15 seconds)
	- Cobra Stretch (Yoga Position)
	- Down Dog Stretch (Yoga Pose)
	- Squat Sit Stretch (10-15 seconds)
	- One Burpee
	- a few Jumping Jacks


2.  Round 1
	- 1 Pull Up
    - 1 Push Up
    - 1 slow Dive-Bomber Push Up
    - 1 slow Full ROM Squat
    - 1 Burpee
    -  5 Jumping Jacks

... continue to Round 5
